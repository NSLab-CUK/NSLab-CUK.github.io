We are thrilled to announce that Van Thuy Hoang, who recently earned his doctoral degree from the Network Science Lab (NS Lab), has received the Excellence Award (우수상) at [the 23rd Graduate School Academic Awards (제23회 대학원 학술상)](https://www.catholic.ac.kr/ko/newsroom/photonews.do?mode=view&articleNo=269469&article.offset=0&articleLimit=16) ceremony.

His award-winning research, titled "Mitigating Degree Bias in Graph Representation Learning With Learnable Structural Augmentation and Structural Self-Attention," addresses critical challenges in Graph ML by proposing a novel framework to mitigate degree bias in graph representations.

Following his Best Award (최우수상) at the 22nd awards last year, this consecutive recognition highlights NS Lab's continuous leadership and academic excellence in the field of Graph Neural Networks (GNNs). Congratulations to Van Thuy Hoang and Professor Oh-Jun Kwon for this outstanding achievement!

NS Lab will continue to push the boundaries of Graph AI and lead the future of intelligent systems.

Recipients of the 23rd Graduate School Academic Awards (제23회 대학원 학술상) are as follows:
* Van Thuy Hoang (Advisor: Prof. O-Joun Lee), “The Excellence Award (우수상)” for “Mitigating Degree Bias in Graph Representation Learning With Learnable Structural Augmentation and Structural Self-Attention”
* Hyeonseung Lee (Advisor: Prof. Kun Na), “The Excellence Award (우수상)” for “Photo-responsive self-expanding catheter with photosensitizer-integrated silicone-covered membrane for minimally invasive local therapy in malignant esophageal cancer”
* Jiyoung Choi (Advisor: Prof. Daehun Park), “The Excellence Award (우수상)” for “Emerging roles of ATG9/ATG9A in autophagy: implications for cell and neurobiology”
* Seongjun Yang (Advisor: Prof. Kang Hoon Lee), “The Excellence Award (우수상)” for “Machine learning based prediction of waste activated sludge generation for optimization of WWTP operational efficiency”
