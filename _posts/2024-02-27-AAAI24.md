Our team member, [Van Thuy Hoang](https://nslab-cuk.github.io/member/hoangvanthuy90), presented his paper, titled "Transitivity-Preserving Graph Representation Learning for Bridging Local Connectivity and Role-based Similarity," at [the 38th AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI 2024)](https://aaai.org/aaai-conference/). This recognition demonstrated his dedication and expertise in graph learning.

* Van Thuy Hoang, O-Joun Lee: Transitivity-Preserving Graph Representation Learning for Bridging Local Connectivity and Role-based Similarity. The 38th AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI 2024), Vancouver, Canada; 02/2024.
